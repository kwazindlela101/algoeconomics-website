About AlgoEconomics

Transforming economic intelligence through advanced algorithms and data-driven insights

Our Mission

AlgoEconomics delivers premium, real-time economic intelligence for UK–Africa decision-makers. It blends automation, strategic segmentation, and modular design to help clients act faster, communicate smarter, and lead with data.

Founded with a vision to spotlight Africa's economic potential and bridge global insights with local relevance, AlgoEconomics is building the trusted intelligence platform for the next generation of leaders, investors, and changemakers.

What the AI Corruption Index Does

Advanced detection and prediction capabilities for economic governance

Detection & Analysis

  • Predicts Corruption Hotspots in public fund allocation — detects anomalies in budgeting, procurement, and disbursement to spotlight high-risk institutions and actors.
  • Identifies Lower-Level Corruption in public service delivery — simplifies procedures and promotes access to accurate information to reduce entry-point corruption.
